Common questions

What data do you store?

Clariva processes your call audio to extract deal signals but does not store recordings. Only structured data - field values, next steps, objections, signals - is retained and synced to your CRM. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never use your call data to train models or share it with third parties.

How long does it take to set up?

No additional setup required before each call. Clariva connects to your calendar and automatically knows when a call starts. Your rep doesn't need to do anything - it works in the background from the first call onwards.

What CRMs do you support?

Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, and more. If your CRM isn't on the list, reach out - we're expanding integrations actively.

Is there a minimum team size?

No minimum. You can start with one rep and expand as you see fit.

We're already using Gong / Chorus. How is Clariva different?

Those tools record and transcribe calls - useful for coaching and reviewing conversations. Clariva is focused on one specific outcome: keeping your CRM accurate without rep effort. No bloat, no extra workflows, no new tabs to check. Just clean, complete data in your pipeline after every call.

What happens to the 50% discount after the design partner phase?

It's locked in permanently. Whatever we charge in the future, design partners always pay half - no renegotiation, no conditions.
