Common questions

What data do you store?

Clariva processes your call audio to extract deal signals and coaching insights but does not store recordings. Only structured data — field values, next steps, objections, call scores, skill signals — is retained and synced to your CRM. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never use your call data to train models or share it with third parties.

How long does it take to set up?

No additional setup required before each call. Clariva connects to your calendar and automatically knows when a call starts. Your rep doesn't need to do anything — it works in the background from the first call onwards.

What CRMs do you support?

Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, and more. If your CRM isn't on the list, reach out — we're expanding integrations actively.

Is there a minimum team size?

No minimum. You can start with one rep and expand as you see fit.

We're already using Gong / Chorus / Avoma. How is Clariva different?

Those tools record and transcribe calls — useful for coaching and reviewing conversations. Clariva is focused on two specific outcomes: keeping your CRM accurate without rep effort, and coaching every rep automatically after every call. No manual scorecard filling. No waiting for a manager to listen. Just clean pipeline data and consistent rep improvement — after every single call.

How does call scoring work?

After every call, Clariva analyses the conversation and scores the rep across key dimensions — discovery, objection handling, value articulation, next step clarity, and talk ratio. The rep gets a debrief immediately: what went well, what to improve, and one specific thing to do differently next time. No manager input required.

What does the manager dashboard show?

A real-time view of your team's skill development. A heatmap showing each rep's strengths and weak spots across key sales dimensions, which calls have the highest coaching impact, which reps are improving and which are sliding — updated after every call. So you always know where to focus your time, without listening to a single recording.

What happens to the 50% discount after the design partner phase?

It's locked in permanently. Whatever we charge in the future, design partners always pay half — no renegotiation, no conditions.
